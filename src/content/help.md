# Bohosaaz Help & Policies

## Orders & Tracking
- You can track your orders from **Account → Orders** after logging in.
- If you have an order ID, keep it ready when contacting support.
- Delivery status updates depend on the courier scan events.

## Shipping
- Standard shipping timelines vary by location and product availability.
- Some products may ship from vendors directly and can have different dispatch times.
- If a product is in stock, it usually dispatches sooner than pre-order/out-of-stock items.

## Returns & Refunds
- Returns are supported for eligible items within the allowed return window.
- Return eligibility depends on product category and the product’s return policy (if specified).
- Refunds are processed after the return is approved/picked and may take additional banking time to reflect.
- For damaged/incorrect items, contact support as soon as possible with photos.

## Coupons
- Coupons can be applied during checkout.
- Some coupons apply to all products, while others apply only to a category or a specific product.
- Coupons may have minimum order amounts, usage limits, and validity windows.

## Vendor Apply & KYC
- To become a vendor, create an account and apply from the **Seller/Vendor** area.
- KYC typically requires business/identity details and supporting documents.
- Vendor applications may be reviewed before approval.

## Contact & Support
- For general questions, use the Contact page.
- For order issues (delivery, returns, refunds), provide your order ID and a short description.
- If you’re logged in, you can also raise a ticket from your account (where available).

## Troubleshooting
- Login issues: confirm you’re using the correct email and reset your password if needed.
- Checkout issues: try clearing the cart and re-adding items, and ensure address/phone details are valid.
- Payment issues: wait a few minutes and refresh; if a payment failed, try again or choose COD (if available).
